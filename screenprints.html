Cats Need Baths


Silkscreen | 11" x 17" | edition of 8

The idea of this print comes from a conversation in class about whether cats need to be washed. I wash my cats regularly, usually once every two months, but my friends from other backgrounds are so surprised about this. The interesting discovery of this subtle difference on habits and thoughts among people captures me so I take it as my topic and develop this set of prints.
