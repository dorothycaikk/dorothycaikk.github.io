Digital Equity Walk


The Digital Equity Walk is an activity held by the Massachusetts Business Alliance for Education, whose initiative is to close the digital equity gap in Massachusetts. By inviting stakeholders to participate and interact with the data, the Digital Equity Walk provides an opportunity to raise awareness and increase audience understanding of the situation that the access to computer science coursework in Massachusetts is inadequate and demographic disparities exist. The event inspires participants to share ideas and take measures to ensure equitable access to computer science for all students in Massachusetts.

As a design fellow at BU Spark!, a student innovation center in Boston University, I teamed up with my colleague Bliss Parsons to work on this project. We reviewed all data sets and information assembled by MBAE, sketched narratives, and finally generated digital and print-ready visual presentations of the data.
