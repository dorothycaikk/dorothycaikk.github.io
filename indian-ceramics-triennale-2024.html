Indian Ceramics Triennale 2024


Common Ground 2024 is the second edition of the Indian Ceramics Triennale. This iteration of the Triennale will be held in New Delhi, India in January 2024.

In early January of 2023, 200 Million Artisans reached out to me and asked me to design the open call posters for this upcoming edition of the Triennale. I was honored to contribute to this meaningful event. In this design, I played with the shapes and colors of the logo and used a grid system to organize the text. The flickering color blocks with the Indian music on the background added a sense of playfulness to the motion posters and delivered a lively vibe. Wish Common Ground 2024 a grand success and wish all great artists and works to be seen.
