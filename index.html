Hello! Ziqi Cai is a passionate graphic designer and illustrator currently residing in Ellicott City, Maryland. With a keen eye for detail and a love for telling stories in visual formats, she specializes in transforming ideas into visually elegant and impactful designs. Her work spans branding, packaging, illustration, type design, and digital experience. She previously worked at NetEast Games and earned her MFA in Graphic Design at Boston University. You could reach her at dotzcai@gmail.com.
