Work About

Little Nature


Little Nature is a Shanghai-based music therapy studio, whose major services include meditation, reiki, and chanting. Little Nature aims to help people reclaim their inner peace and feel the connection with nature by inviting them into a calming environment and interacting with natural musical instruments. Compared to the grandness of the outer world, our little inner serenity will help us find a true sense of security and be loyal to ourselves.

To deliver Little Nature’s vision of life, the design was built around the key message, “Create the abundance of life from emptiness.” The solid and misty effects of the brand colors release the soul of the brand. The modified stokes of the Chinese characters in the logo present a figurative scene of nature as Chinese characters are a mixture of pictograms, ideograms, and phono-semantic compounds. Since mindfulness is still not a prevalent idea in China, I created an illustration for people to have a general idea of what experience Little Nature can offer them.
