Super Mecha Champions


Super Mecha Champions is a third-person, mecha battle royale game, with urban comic style developed by NetEase Games. In this game you will gather in Alpha City as a mecha pilot and fight against other players to claim the top of the mecha championship.

Working with the game design team and the marketing team, we built new visual guidelines for the game, making its visual system more consolidated and refreshing.

I was in charge of leading early stage research, aligning design ideas and directions, and creating visual propopsals with the team. The new designs were developed as editable templates that the marketing team can follow and modify for introducing new characters and new skins.
