Niu Year Gift Box


2021 is the year of the Ox. People born during a year of the Ox are known for being diligent, dependable, strong, and determined. The client intended to launch a new year gift box for students to promote their stationery supplies and to send their best wishes to all students for the new school year. I created an illustration and designed the collateral products for them. Due to unexpected changes to their marketing strategy, the gift box did not come out on the market. But it was a wonderful experience and we continue to establish more future cooperations.
