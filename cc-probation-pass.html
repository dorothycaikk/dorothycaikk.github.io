CC Probation Pass


CC (Creative Commons) is one of the Art & Design Departments in NetEase Games. They have a high standard for the quality of everything that they produce, no matter it is for external use or internal use. In this task, I was responsible for the entire design of the probation pass, from ideation, illustration, layouts, paper selection, to communication with the printing house.

The final design is neat and straightforward. The metallic texture of the paper reflects the goal of the team, which is to be "Efficient, Professional, and Extreme". The embossment on the cover shows a sense of detail and delicacy.
