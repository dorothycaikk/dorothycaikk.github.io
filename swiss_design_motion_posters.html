Swiss design Motion Posters


This is a personal iterative redesign practice of a beautiful Swiss design poster, which was released around 2008. The original poster is simple and strong. It shows the power of the International Style, which favored simplicity, legibility, and objectivity. The concept of this iterative practice is dynamic evolution. So I redesigned the poster fifty times and then edited them into a video and a series of animated posters as final deliverables.
