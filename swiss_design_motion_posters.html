Swiss design Motion Posters


This is a personal iterative re-design practice of a beautiful Swiss design poster, which was released in around 2008. The image of the original poster is simple and strong. It shows the power of the International Style, which favored simplicity, legibility, and objectivity. The iterations are designed based on the idea of dynamic evolution, so they were edited into a video and a series of animated posters as final deliverables.

Instructor: Kristen Coogan
